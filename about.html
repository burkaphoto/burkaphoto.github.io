Burka Photography is the photography business created in 2015 by Conor Burke, my goal is to create beautiful images as an artform and create lasting memories for my customers by capturing their most precious moments. I believe photography as an artform allows for the preservation of culture. I take photographs in all different themes, ranging from modelling photography, product shots, animal photography, wedding photography and all types of other services.
My aim is to build up the Burka Photography brand and bring on more photographers, videographers and other creatives to broaden out my roster and produce more and better content.
